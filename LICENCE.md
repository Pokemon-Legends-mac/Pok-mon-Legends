## 🕹️ Gameplay Overview

In **Pokémon Legends**, players venture across diverse biomes, including forests, mountains, rivers, and snowy landscapes. Unlike traditional Pokémon games, trainers can freely roam the world, gather resources, craft useful items, and interact with Pokémon in real time.

Players can choose to sneak through tall grass, strategically throw Poké Balls, or initiate battles to capture and study Pokémon. Completing research tasks contributes to building a comprehensive Pokédex and advancing the story.
